BEYOND BEACHES

Island experiences that take you out of the water.

DINE WITH THE CHEF IN CANCUN

Join resort chefs in the Culinary Center to learn the secrets behind Mexican and Italian cuisines. Hands-on classes are offered throughout the week, and meals are paired with craft cocktails and wine. Kids classes are also available.

SET SAIL IN GRAND CAYMAN

Discover historical shipwrecks, colorful coral reefs and an array of marine life aboard a state-of-the-art hydrofoil boat. A six-foot, curved-glass dome magnifies everything under the sea to twice its size.

TAKE A HIKE IN DORADO BEACH

Amid the resort’s 50 acres, the 11-mile Rockefeller Nature Trail can be explored by bike, foot or golf cart and offers a changing landscape that takes in the Atlantic Ocean, tree-shaded forests and two golf courses.

PLAY A ROUND IN ARUBA

Designed by Robert Trent Jones II, the resort’s 18 holes use wind, earth and sea to create challenges for golfers of all skill levels. Private lessons, clinics, a pro shop, driving range and restaurant are also offered.
