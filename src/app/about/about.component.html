How it All Started

How it all started This initiative came as a natural development in today’s technologically led marketplace. There are more than 500 marinas all over the london where millions of boats are mooring every month. Nevertheless, there have been just a few limited online solutions for skippers to book berths and extra facilities alike. Moreover, marinas have been missing an online hub where they can discover and engage new customers. Perceiving this context as a business opportunity, our professional team with over 6 years of experience in the nautical field launched MB Solutions. Here, any sailing enthusiast or sea traveller can get accurate and useful information about the main attractions in the marinas' venues and regions. At the same time, marina owners can list their facilities, providing detailed information about what makes their harbours unique.
